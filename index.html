Reviving the Dai Tradition

For centuries, Indian families relied on the dai, the kaki, the masi—women who provided continuous emotional, physical, and informational support. Colonial-era medicalization replaced this woman-centered care with clinical efficiency.

We are reclaiming this heritage. Our platform gives this ancient wisdom structure, safety, and scale for the modern urban mother.
